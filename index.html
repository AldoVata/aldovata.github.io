My current featured project focuses on sports analytics, causal inference, and visual data storytelling.

VAR and Football Attendance

An empirical senior thesis analyzing whether the introduction of VAR affected live football attendance in the English Premier League compared with the English Championship.

The project uses Difference-in-Differences regression, football performance controls, attendance data, and interactive visualizations to explain how VAR may have shaped attendance patterns and match dynamics after 2019.
