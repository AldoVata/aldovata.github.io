VAR and Football Attendance

An empirical senior thesis analyzing whether the introduction of Video Assistant Referee technology changed live football attendance in the English Premier League compared with the English Championship.

View GitHub Repository View Visuals

Research Question

Did the introduction of VAR reduce live football attendance? This project uses a Difference-in-Differences design comparing Premier League teams affected by VAR after 2019 with Championship teams that did not use VAR during the same period.

Treatment Group

Premier League teams after VAR was introduced in the 2019/20 season.

Control Group

English Championship teams that did not use VAR during the same comparison period.

Main Outcome

Average stadium attendance by team and season.

Key Findings

VAR Attendance Effect

VAR was associated with a decline of about 1,233 fans, but the effect was not statistically significant at conventional levels.

Offsides Changed Significantly

Offsides per 90 showed the strongest statistically significant change after VAR.

COVID Complication

Attendance trends around 2020 are heavily affected by COVID-19 restrictions, making interpretation more difficult.
